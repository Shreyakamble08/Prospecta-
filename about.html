Our Story

Founded in 2018, Prospecta BuildX India Pvt. Ltd. is a Pune-based EPC and general contracting company delivering high-quality, timely, and cost-effective construction solutions across India. From industrial and warehousing projects to commercial constructions, we have become a trusted partner for leading brands and developers.

Our expertise covers end-to-end EPC services from engineering and procurement to construction and commissioning ensuring transparency, accountability, and seamless execution. Every project reflects our commitment to precision, safety, and efficiency, delivering real value to our clients.

By embracing modern technologies and sustainable practices, Prospecta BuildX builds not just structures, but lasting trust for today and tomorrow.
